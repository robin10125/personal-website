Alchemy Photowall

I made a photowall app for an event I ran.

I made a photowall app that allows users to upload photos they took at my event and view other's photos, on a single shared photowall. Much of the work in this project was in hosting the app on AWS. I was on an intense schedule, planning this event, so developing this app in the margins of free time I had was deceptively difficult.

I made an admin mode to allow us to review and approve uploaded photos before they were displayed on the public wall. The event has ended so I am no longer maintaining the website hosting on AWS, but you can check out the repo here.

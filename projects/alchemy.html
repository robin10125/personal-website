Alchemy

I hosted a creator event name Alchemy, with over 250 attendees, managing many, many, many responsibilites.

Alchemy is a demo day my fellow hosts and I organized and ran for the London community. We started planning in May 2025, and continued until March 2026. We organized over 30 demos of student and recent grad's passion projects, with projects like tech startups, AI hardware, viral social media brands, dance organizations, writers, 3d printed art, amongst many more.. I was invloved in pretty much every aspect of organizing this event. It was a big success, with over 250 attendees, which is more than double the number of the previous year. We are back at it again, preparing an even bigger and better demo day next year.

Alchemy

I hosted a creator event name Alchemy, with over 250 attendees, managing many, many, many responsibilities.

Alchemy is a demo day my fellow hosts and I organized and ran for the London community. We started planning in May 2025, and continued until March 2026. We organized over 30 demos of student and recent grad's passion projects, with projects like tech startups, AI hardware, viral social media brands, dance organizations, writers, 3d printed art, amongst many more..

I was involved in pretty much every aspect of organizing this event, from organizing demos and demoers, to managing finances, social media, communications, logistics and much, much more.

It was a big success, with over 250 attendees, which is more than double the number of the previous year.

The main goal was to leave attendees inspired to start and work on their own passion projects, and make them realize they could do things they didn't even concieve of, let alone think possible.

It was our contribution to the growing culture of creator communities all over world.

We are back at it again -preparing an even bigger and better demo day for next year.

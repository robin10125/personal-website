My Favourite Papers/Projects

  • Eureka - This project uses LLMs to design reward code for robotic agents. When I first read this it inspired me greatly, and continues to inspire me to this day. What I think is most important about this is using AI to essentially guide the training of AI.
  • Attention is All You Need - The Transformer paper. Transformers have become the foundation of almost all modern AI, including LLMs, generative images, and speech to text. This paper provides a clear and easy to read outline of the Transformer architecture, and it's important attention mechanism. Note that this paper uses "Post Normalization", meaning that normalization layers are after the attention layers, whereas most implementations use "Pre Norm", placing normalization layers before the attention layers.
  • No Prop - Training Neural Networks without Full Back-propagation or Full Forward-propagation.

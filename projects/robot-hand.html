Robot Hand

I made a robot hand from scratch as an introduction to computer science and technical projects.

I was bored of my medical sciences coursework, so I decieded to start on a project to introduce myself to robotics, computer science and other technical fields. I designed the hand from scratch in OnShape, 3d printed it, and assembled it with servos to move the digits. I programmed the micro controller to control the servers, and used skin electrodes that detect muscle contraction as the input device.

Unfortunately I have lost the hand before I could document the finished product.
